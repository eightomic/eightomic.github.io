About

I'm William Stafford Parsons, a long-time software engineer turned computer science researcher and minimalist game developer.

Video games are a perfect blend of art and science, expressive and functional, with one either an asset or a bottleneck to the other.

Creating them opens a rare opportunity to craft experiences to enhance a few moments of someone's life.

I bring minimalist video games with high-quality, smooth gameplay to modern desktop web browsers using pure CSS, HTML and JavaScript, my favorite programming language.

Each game uses only 8 keys on the keyboard, specifically the arrow keys and w a s d.

They're fun for short bursts of time with intuitive controls and super-polished gameplay to preserve the feeling of wanting to insert a quarter into an arcade machine just to experience 5 minutes of a video game.

I respect that you may have a busy, stressful life outside of gaming and may already have hundreds of other games to grind, so each game is built without HUDs, XP grinding, achievement hunting, in-game currency purchases, objectives, loot boxes, malicious downloads or player death.

As a result, I'm able to focus on creating fun games where the arcade-style, high-quality, meditative gameplay experience is the only reward system.

If I don't have fun playing the games I create, I don't expect others to enjoy playing them. Therefore, my games are the only games that I play.

Furthermore, I have an uncommon affinity for noticing patterns and unconventional approaches to common problems, so I conduct open computer science research in the area of creating new fundamental algorithms.

The results from my research are capable of setting new software engineering standards for efficient, high-performance, practical computing when applied to real-world systems, such as databases, internet network infrastructure and web browser JavaScript engines.

My dream is to eventually earn a living by doing exactly what I'm doing because I'd do it regardless.

If you'd like to send me a donation to support my work, I don't have a bank account, a cryptocurrency wallet, a mobile phone or a photo ID.
