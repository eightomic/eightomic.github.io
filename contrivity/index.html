Explanation

This game was designed as a fun, minimalist, relaxing shoot-em-up game to unleash carnage upon zombie-like enemies without stress triggers from challenging objectives, player death, realistic visuals or sound.

It's heavily-optimized with a proprietary game engine using vanilla JavaScript, HTML and CSS to ensure optimal functionality across the widest range of modern web browsers and computers as possible.

As with all creations, there was some inspiration from others.

The attention to detail and high framerate from the Zed Time system in Killing Floor 2 inspired the inclusion of a high-quality bullet time "slow-mo" system triggered once at random after each weapon switch.

The frantic twin-stick arena shooting controls and simplistic shapes from Geometry Wars inspired the core gameplay premise with limited 8-directional, arcade-style movement and minimal graphics using only circles and lines.

The pick-up-and-play gameplay style with quick pacing changes from the WarioWare series inspired the inclusion of rapid, randomized weapon switching between short-lived waves of enemies to reduce boredom from repetition.

There are 15 in-game weapons so far in Contrivity with many more carefully-crafted weapons planned for future updates.
